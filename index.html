About Our Cafe

Welcome to Alsdorf Park Public Boat Launch, your go-to destination for an enjoyable and convenient boating experience. Nestled in a picturesque setting, our launch offers effortless access to the sparkling waters that surround us, ideal for fishing enthusiasts, water sports lovers, and leisurely boating adventures. At Alsdorf Park, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch facilities, including advanced JDL (Jet Drive Launch), LDD (Launch Docking Drive), and DSL (Dual Service Launch) options, ensuring that your boat is launched and retrieved with maximum efficiency and minimal wait times. We believe that a great day on the water begins with a great launch experience.

Our well-maintained park features ample parking for vehicles with trailers, so you can spend less time worrying about logistics and more time enjoying your day out. With clean restrooms, picnic areas for post-launch relaxation, and a dedicated staff ready to assist, Alsdorf Park Public Boat Launch is committed to making your visit as enjoyable as possible. Come join us for your next adventure on the water and experience first-hand the convenience and beauty that Alsdorf Park has to offer!
